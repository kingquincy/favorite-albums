Dark Sky Paradise

Artist : Big Sean AKA Sean Anderson

Release Date : February 24, 2015

Favorite Song : One Man Can Change the World featuring Kanye West and John Legend

Background : Dark Sky Paradise received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised the change in Sean's production and lyricism being more atmospheric and substance-filled. The album debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200, earning 173,000 album-equivalent units in its first week. This became Sean's first US number one debut and his highest first week sales to date. In its second week, the album dropped to number six on the chart, earning an additional 58,000 units. In its third week, the album dropped to number ten on the chart, earning 38,000 more units. By the end of 2015, Dark Sky Paradise was ranked as the 22nd most popular album of the year on the Billboard 200. In August 2020, the album was certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America.c
