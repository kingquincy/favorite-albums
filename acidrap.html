Acid Rap

Artist : Chance The Rapper AKA Chancelor Bennett

Release Date : April 30, 2013

Favorite Song : Favorite Song featuring Childish Gambino

Background : The album debuted at number 63 on the Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums, due to bootleg downloads on iTunes and Amazon not affiliated with the artist. The mixtape has been certified "diamond" on mixtape site Datpiff, for garnering over 1,000,000 downloads. The cover art for Acid Rap was based on a real picture taken at South by Southwest, an annual conglomerate of film, interactive media, and music festivals and conferences that take place in mid-March in Austin, Texas. The picture was taken by Brandon Beuax, who also designed the cover art for Bennett's previous mixtape 10 Day. The picture happened by chance, according to Beaux
